Network Infrastructure Management

At MYTSSS, we provide end-to-end Network Infrastructure Solutions to ensure fast, reliable, and secure connectivity for businesses of all sizes. From structured cabling to server room setup and hardware installation, our expert team delivers scalable and future-ready networks tailored to your needs.

We serve clients across India, with a strong focus on performance, efficiency, and compliance with industry standards.

Scope of Services

1. Site Survey & Requirement Assessment

  • On-site inspection to assess existing infrastructure
  • Identification of network needs, bandwidth requirements, and scalability
  • Consultation on best practices for enterprise network design

2. Network Design & Planning

  • Customized network topology (Star, Mesh, Hybrid)
  • IP scheme, hardware selection (switches, routers, patch panels)
  • Structured cabling layout using Cat6/Cat6A/Fiber

3. Cable Laying & Termination

  • LAN, Fiber, UTP/STP cable laying
  • Crimping, punching, and patch panel termination
  • Cable testing and signal certification

4. Server Room Setup

  • Rack installation, power planning, cooling and ventilation
  • Server and NOC setup with surge protection

5. Switch & Router Installation

  • Installation/configuration of Layer 2/3 switches and routers
  • VLANs, QoS, load balancing, and security setup
  • Firewall and access control integration

6. Testing, Commissioning & Documentation

  • Performance testing and optimization
  • Full documentation with labeling and diagrams
  • Admin/user training and handover
